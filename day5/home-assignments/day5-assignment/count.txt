An apple fruit is a delicious and nutritious fruit produced by apple trees that belong to the Rosaceae family. The tree is grown in various parts of the world and has been cultivated for very many years. There are over 7500 varieties of this delicious fruit which come is different colors
specifically yellow, red and green. The fruit has a thin but sturdy skin while the inner flesh is juicy and thick, and softens on ripening. The inner core contains the seeds, which are not good for human consumption.

The nutrients rest in the skin and flesh which happen to be rich sources of tannins and anthocyanin. True to the saying an Apple a day keeps your doctor away, apples have many health benefits. Whether you eat the fruit as a stand-alone snack or as a section of salads, desserts, pies or smoothies, the fruit does not disappoint.
